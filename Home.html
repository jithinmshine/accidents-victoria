Do you know how many accidents happen in Victoria?

Accident Trend in Victoria 2006-2018

Road accidents data is collected by Victorian government to analyze the crash statistics and develop counter measures to decrease the road accidents. VicRoads provides crash statistics to organizations, researchers and the general public to help with education, research and the development of road safety programs and initiatives.

Victoria started the analysis of data from around 2013. And as part of the open data movement, the organization started publishing the datasets from 2014. After they started analyzing the data and implementing the counter measures, we can clearly see a drop in the number of accidents in Victoria from 2014
